ABOUT SNEHA RIDES

Improving lives and Building Futures.

The RIDES (Ravicherla Integrated Development and Education Society) established in the year 1996 under the dynamic leadership of social activities. Shri.B.Nagesh Rao and team of nine committed members from education, medical and rehabilitation services, RIDES ever since its establishment started working for unprivileged and disadvantaged children in Krishna District to protect their rights for basic education, health joyful environment and life skills development training. The RIDES in the year 1988 started providing basic rehabilitation services to different challenge children with disabilities to reduce the effect of disability in their day to day life by providing required Aid/Appliance like tricycle, wheelchair, calipers, hearing aids and artificial limb for ortho disability looking at good work Ministry of social which continuing till today. RIDES received Best NGO award for the same in the year 2002, 2006, 2009 RIDES have provided 648 Aid/Appliances to identified persons with disability till 2015. RIDES have successfully formed 89 active Self Help Groups of person with disabilities.

In the year 2004, after looking at high prevalence of HIV/AIDS infection in Nuzvid division RIDES has started home based care & support project with financial assistance from CRS(Catholic Relief Services) and have reached most remote village to prevent HIV infection among innocent rural children and care & Support by provoding treatment & nutrition help to the identified infected Childrejn. RIDES was only the organization went door to door campaign on prevention of HIV/AIDS and to reach grass root levels to identify new people living with HIV/AIDS and infected children further referral and rehabilitation services. RIDES strongly came to conclusion that large number of children have become orphan with HIV epidemic and children have negative impact in their lives in emotional, educational and health which impact result, many children are force to leave school, to support their families in new situation with little or no support and many girls children have been faced exploitation and abused as they have no protected place to live, looking at need RIDES started “SNEHA RIDES CHILDREN HOME”

For children in need of protected shelter for their institutional educational care. RIDES started with 20 children in the year 2004, in year 2020 we have 108 children, among them 38 children are HIV positive orphans. 12 children are rag pickers children referred child welfare committee and remaining children are semi orphan children RIDES have separated building for girls and boys for their stay.
