SNEHA RIDES CHILDREN HOME

Improving lives and Building Futures

The RIDES (Ravicherla Integrated Development and Education Society) established in the year 1996 under the dynamic leadership of social activities. Shri.B.Nagesh Rao and team of nine committed members from education, medical and rehabilitation services, RIDES ever since its establishment started working for unprivileged and disadvantaged communities in Krishna District to protect their rights for health education development and participation and live in healthy, joyful environment. The RIDES in the year 1998 started providing basic rehabitation services to person with disabilities to reduce the affect of disability in their day to day life by providing require aid/appliance like tricycle, wheelchairs, calipers, hearing aids and artificial limb for ortho disability looking at the good work ministry of social justice and empowerment, Govt of india has provided financial assistance of RIDES.

We Protect child rights for education, health & nutrition and protect child in emergency, prevent integrated comprehensive education and health services to unprivileged and street children in urban slums and disadvantage communities.

WHAT WE DO?

To improve the quality of life and well-being of nation’s most vulnerable children and their households by empowering households and communities to provide comprehensive and sustainable care, support and protection.

Health

Multiple health camps were conducted to identify children’s health problems & link the children to the appropriate services.Camps included:general health checkups to identify chronic illness, screenings for adolescent girls, dental & ophthalmological screenings.

Read More

Education

5,722 children of KP have received highly needed education services, including educational scholarships through the Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS) & educational supplies through private and corporate support.


Read More

Nutrition

We provide nutrition-related services. While the efforts to develop formal linkages to the government run Integrated Child Development Scheme are happening in all states, much of the support in this quarter has been from local donors and community groups.

Read More

Life Skills Education

Adolescents (10-18 years) of were engaged and provided life-skills education that enables them to make choices & avoid exposure to behaviour that makes them unsafe to get HIV. Children have been applied in life-skills education program that enhance their skills to deal with the vulnerabilities they face due to being children of KP.

Read More

Social Security

Providing services to care givers which includes parenting education. Providing access to social protection programs for caregivers children adolescents. This is done through linkage with several corporations, both in Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra, including ONGC, Amazon, DDB Mudra, and local companies.

Read More

HIV Testing & Treatment

We mainly focus on prioritizing the children of HIV positive KPs - testing the never tested children & adolescents manifesting risk behaviour. During last year, many children were found to be eligible for HIV testing based on the positive HIV status of their parents, of which a few children knew their HIV status.
